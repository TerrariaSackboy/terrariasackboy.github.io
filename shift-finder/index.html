About Shift Finder

Shift Finder is a web app that is designed to ease the experience of laying out people's shifts. Currently, it is planned to save user data in the browser's local storage. Give it a try!

Adding People

To add a person to the schedule, just press the plus button.

Saving Schedule Templates

If you have a nice set of times on a person, you can set it as the default by clicking the "..." button and selecting "Save Template". When you make a new schedule, it will automagically fill all the rows from your templates.

Editing Time Templates

When you select one of the main boxes, a dropdown will show up with some selectable default times. If you want to change what appears, choose the "Time Templates" button on the toolbar.

Saving and Loading Schedules

The application autosaves some things, such as the days you hid, the list of names, and their order, whenever you leave. You can load these with the Load Saved Schedule button from the Load button in the toolbar.

You can also export or import the entire schedule through the save and load menus. This is useful if you need to clear your cache for some reason. I forgot to make a button to export templates and other settings though. Coming soon I think.

Printing a Schedule

When you have all the values inputted into the schedule, you can use your browser's print option to get it onto paper, a .pdf file, or whatever else.
